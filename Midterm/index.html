What is Hello Kitty Train?

The Hello Kitty Shinkansen is a special edition bullet train operated by West Japan Railway Company (JR West).

It runs on the Sanyo Shinkansen, connecting Shin-Osaka Station in Osaka to Hakata Station in Fukuoka, offering a delightful travel option across western Japan. Riding the Hello Kitty Shinkansen is more than just taking a train—it's an experience to be enjoyed. It’s an unforgettable adventure into the world of Hello Kitty and the charming cities of western Japan.

Facts About The Hello Kitty Shinkansen:

  • The train's exterior is eye-catching, painted in vibrant pink and adorned with giant Hello Kitty decals.
  • Step into the world of Hello Kitty! Expect charming decorations, Hello Kitty-shaped headrests, and even Hello Kitty-themed announcements.
  • Each carriage features a unique design representing specific regions along the route, showcasing local mascots and Hello Kitty dressed in traditional attire.
  • Purchase exclusive Hello Kitty Shinkansen souvenirs while riding the bullet train.
